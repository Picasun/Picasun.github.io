英语小作文

开头

  • 介绍自己（私人信函不写）
    • I am a senior from the Department of Computer Science in this/a university.
    • I am a staff member of your company.
    • I am a xxx who did sth yesterday.
  • 表达情感
    • My missive , as a matter of fact/on behalf of xx,seeks to express/do
      • my sincere/heartfelt apology/appreciation/congratulations/welcome（感谢/道歉/祝贺/欢迎）to you
      • my dissatisfaction about（投诉）
      • inquire whether you could do（求助/咨询）
      • offer some practical proposals about xx to you（建议/倡议）
      • invite you to xxxx （邀请信）
      • resign from my current position and make a sincere apology for any inconvenience.
      • recommend xxx as xxx
    • In response to your last letter，

中间

  • It , to begin with, seems my argument that +not only butalso 倒装句
  • In addition，+so that 倒装句
  • Another element I must point out is that xxx,which has been ignored by numorous xxxx.

结尾

  • 感谢
    • I’d like to extend my greatest appreciation if you are so kind as to take my suggestions into consideration/accept my apology.
    • Thanks again for your help/time.
    • Please accept my apology for any convenienve caused and thank you for your understanding.
  • 期待回信
    • I’m looking forward to hearing from you in the near future.
  • 保持联系
    • Don’t hesitate to/Feel free to/Please
      • contact me if you need further help/have further question/I can be of any assistance.

英语大作文

开头段落：

  • 公式：套话+主题+变化+程度+时间范围
  • As is （vividly/subtly） revealed in the（Line chart线/bar chart柱、条/pie chart饼/table表）above，we can easily notice that the proportion of xxx had increased to .On the contrary ,the amount of xxx was in decline and drooped from xx to xx.
  • As is known to all，A is a thought-provoking / highly controversial issue with which (this society/citizens) is confronted when the past couple of years have witnessed its growing （popularity/gravity）.

中间段落：

  • There are a couple of driving forces, I would argue,

    • behind this （undesirable / desirable）（proportion、tendency）.

  • To begin with，

    • A ,which is a must,xxx
      • helps me develop a more mature outlook on xxx
      • playing an indispensable role in our daily life.
    • To be specific，the more we know the consciousness of xxx，the more likely we are to xxx

  • In addition,

    • it is apparent that A is conducive to xxxx,

    • with diverse factors taken into consideration.

    • is closely tied to the economic boom(economic development/the consciousness of),

    • b and c

  • Ultimately, with the absence of A,

    • xxx would be caught up in the vicious circle, thus jeopardizing our golden opportunities to xxxx.

结尾段落：

  • To put it in a nutshell, so far-reaching is A that nobody could afford to ignore the significance, especially our youngsters.
  • It is high time that we gave the top priority to A.
  • Personally, if this is so, I deem that we will see our shared efforts pay off with a promising and prosperous future awaiting us.
