This experience made the practice more fascinating for me, which also built up my confidence during the exam preparation. Even as a major subject in the examinations, learning computer networks is not a big problem for me because what I had learned in the “Packet Capture Experiment” was carried into practice throughout the whole process. I believe that all these experiences have also laid a solid foundation for me to learn about network security in the future.

I believe that life is precious and it is important to seize any opportunity to improve myself, especially in this competitive world. Therefore, I want to further my study at East China Normal University you know.

Maybe my idea now is to learn professional skills well during my postgraduate studies and join the workforce after three years.After all, I took the professional master’s degree not the academic master’s degree.
