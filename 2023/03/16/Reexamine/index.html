English self-introduction

  • Greetings
    • Good morning, dear professors.I am very honored to give my introduction here. My name is Sun Zhiqiang. I was born in Shanghai and grew up here. Currently, I’m in my senior year at Shanghai University, majoring in Computer Science.
  • College Life + Daily Study
    • My college life means a lot for my personal growth. During my four-year stay in school, I kept up my study pace and received perfect scores in most of my computer science courses. Thanks to my hard work, I have received several academic scholarships. In the meantime, I have been actively involved in various internships and volunteer activities while maintaining my academic performance. These experiences, like software development internships and volunteering at the World AI Conference, not only brought me school-level scholarships but also broadened my understanding of the whole computer industry. Then I realized the significance of keeping knowledge acquired, which can boost both my enthusiasm and attainment in academic research. That was the time I found out that if I could purchase further education at a higher academic institution, I would attain enlightenment of cutting-edge technologies and advance my academic learning and research.
  • Professional study in university
    • In my junior year, I found my own interest in the professional field and started to learn about network security-related knowledge and protocols, such as the stateless feature of HTTP and the TCP/IP protocol stack. Once, in order to finish an important assignment about course：Database Principles, I added a lot of network security-related content to the login screen, such as cookies, sessions, leak buckets, and other mechanisms. This experience made the practice more fascinating for me, which also built up my confidence during the exam preparation. Even as a major subject in the examinations, learning computer networks is not a big problem for me because what I had learned in the “Packet Capture Experiment” was carried into practice throughout the whole process. I believe that all these experiences have also laid a solid foundation for me to learn about network security in the future.
  • Personality
    • Throughout the previous four years, I have acquired a great deal of professional knowledge and practical abilities, but I have gradually realized that this is not enough. In my opinion, further education is actually the urgent need for me to achieve and eventually realize my self-worth. I am a cheerful, hard-working and efficiency-seeking student. I believe that life is precious and it is important to seize any opportunity to improve myself, especially in this competitive world. Therefore, I want to further my study at East China Normal University you know.
  • Plan
    • If I were given the chance to be a post-graduate,I’m going to focus on my major in my first year, finding an area that interests me and where I can produce results under the guidance of my supervisor.In the second year, I will prepare for thesis, practice and writing.In my final year, I will consider various factors and decide my future path by collecting information in advance and seeking advice from my supervisors and professors. Maybe my idea now is to learn professional skills well during my postgraduate studies and join the workforce after three years.After all, I took the professional master’s degree not the academic master’s degree.
  • Ending
    • I am very eager to become a postgraduate student here, not only because of the valuable opportunity provided by this interview,but also it is a once-in-a-lifetime impetus for my life journey. And I hope that the coming three-year learning experience at your school will allow me to become an academic scholar who can make a difference in one field. That’s all for my introduction. Thank you for listening.
